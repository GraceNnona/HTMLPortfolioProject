Developing With a Passion While Exploring The World.

My name is Grace Nnona, and I am an exceptional individual who embodies academic excellence, resilience, and an unwavering passion for growth.
I hail from Nkanu West Local Government Area in Enugu State. I graduated with a degree in Applied Microbiology and Brewing from Enugu State University of Science and Technology.
After completing my studies, I worked as a microbiologist for over a year at the Nigerian Ports Authority in Marina, Lagos.
This experience provided me with valuable exposure to the field. However, I felt a strong desire to explore the world of technology.
After conducting extensive research on various aspects of tech, I discovered software development, which resonated deeply with me.
As a result, I decided to pursue further studies in software development at GOMYCODE in Nigeria.
After then, the journey has been nothing short of blissful.
